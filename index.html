    Zerodha Kite, Upstox, Angel One, Fyers, Dhan, IBKR, Alpaca, and TradingView alerts. Other broker APIs can be added on request.

    Yes — for signal scoring, pattern classification, and feature engineering. We keep models transparent and always paired with explicit risk rules.

    No. TradeMind AI is a software development service. We build the trading infrastructure; you own the strategy and the risk. Markets are inherently uncertain.

    Yes — you receive full source code, docs, and a maintenance window after launch. Extended support and upgrades can be quoted separately.
